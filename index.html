User Guide

Getting Started

  1. Access the Dashboard

    Click "Get Started" from the welcome screen or use the Home button in the bottom navigation to view your dashboard with quick stats and category cards.

  2. Navigate the Dashboard

    The home screen shows your quick stats and category cards. Tap any category to view details.

  3. Add Entries

    Inside each category, tap the + button to add new entries.

Health Tracker

Track your fitness journey and overall wellness

  • Weight: Log your weight daily to track trends
  • Exercise: Record workouts with duration and type
  • Water Intake: Track glasses of water (goal: 8/day)
  • Sleep: Monitor sleep hours for better rest
üí° Tip: Consistency is key! Try to log daily for best results.

Diet Tracker

Monitor your nutrition and eating habits

  • Meals: Log breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks
  • Calories: Track caloric intake per meal
  • Macros: Record protein, carbs, and fats
  • Notes: Add details about ingredients or feelings
üí° Tip: Take photos of meals for visual tracking!

Schedule Manager

Organize your daily tasks and events

  • Tasks: Create to-do items with priorities
  • Events: Schedule activities with time slots
  • Reminders: Set notifications for important items
  • Categories: Tag items as work, personal, etc.
üí° Tip: Review your schedule each morning to stay organized!

Appointments

Never miss important meetings and checkups

  • Medical: Doctor visits, checkups, prescriptions
  • Professional: Work meetings, consultations
  • Personal: Haircuts, vet visits, etc.
  • Notifications: Get reminded before appointments
üí° Tip: Add location and doctor notes for medical appointments!
